ABOUT US

Social connection is the experience of feeling close and connected to others. It involves feeling loved, cared for, and valued, and forms the basis of interpersonal relationships.



Talkpack was founded on the belief that meaningful connections can be made online, and our team is dedicated to creating a platform that fosters community, inclusivity, and creativity. Our mission is to provide a safe and welcoming space for users to express themselves, share ideas, and build lasting relationships. We prioritize community, creativity, and empathy, and our diverse team of innovators, designers, and problem-solvers work tirelessly to make Talkpack the best it can be. From humble beginnings to a global community, we're committed to continuously improving and evolving to meet the needs of our users. Increasingly, social connection is understood as a core human need, and the desire to connect as a fundamental drive.It is crucial to development; without it, social animals experience distress and face severe developmental consequences. In humans, one of the most social species, social connection is essential to nearly every aspect of health and well-being. Lack of connection, or loneliness, has been linked to inflammation,accelerated aging and cardiovascular health risk, suicide, and all-cause mortality.

Feeling socially connected depends on the quality and number of meaningful relationships one has with family, friends, and acquaintances. Going beyond the individual level, it also involves a feeling of connecting to a larger community. Connectedness on a community level has profound benefits for both individuals and society.
